FAQ
What is the purpose of Dirty Laundry?
DirtyLaundry is a website designed to allow users to review each other. This prevents users from hiding their true colors. Here people pay for their behavior. Never make friendships blind again!
How do I navigate the cite?
The navigation bar is always present at the top of the page to offer quick access around the cite. The homepage, friendspage, search and more are all just one click away.
How do I find someone?
To find your friends, simply click on the friends link in the navigation bar and search through the list. To find other users, click on the search link in the navigation bar. Then, fill in the search form to find related users.
How do I review someone?
To review a user, make your way to their page. In the review section of their page, there is a write review button in the top right corner. Click on it, fill out the form, and enter your review.
Can I put anything in a review?
The only limits in the review are no external links, no profanity, and no sexual or drug references. Otherwise, the review is open ended, praise him as much as possible or make them pay!
Can I remove a review from my page?
Reviews on your page are out of your control. Users are free to write anything they want about you. Unless hey violate a rule mentioned in the previous question, there is nothing you can do. If they have violated the terms, report a violation through the report link in the navigation bar.
